Project: Multilingual Video Presentation - "Exploring Sebtoon: A Hallyu Marvel"
- Independently produced and presented a captivating multilingual video for a Listening and Speaking class, delving into the Hallyu phenomenon.
- Selected the intriguing topic of Sebtoon, a prominent Hallyu wave in Thailand, to explain the essence of Hallyu and its impact.
- Skillfully utilized video editing tools to shoot and edit engaging clips using a mobile application, showcasing multimedia proficiency.
- Spoke fluently in Korean while incorporating bilingual subtitles (Korean and Thai) to ensure accessibility and understanding.
- Introduced Sebtoon as a significant aspect of Hallyu, emphasizing its popularity among Thai audiences.
- Recommended and provided insightful commentary on three favorite Sebtoons, enhancing cross-cultural appreciation.
- Uploaded the video on YouTube, contributing to the digital dissemination of Hallyu content and fostering language inclusivity in the Thai-speaking community.
